Tofu Poke Bowl★

  • 2 servings
  • vegetarian
  • salty

Main dish.

Ingredients

  • 16oz extra firm tofu
  • 1cup rice(maybe more)
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1-2 Jalapenos
  • green onions
  • bean sprouts
  • Brocoli
  • 1tbsp Tamari Sauce(Maybe less I honestly just wing it)
  • Wasabi sauce
  • Spicy Mayo Sauce
  • soy sauce

Instructions

Start rice and chop veggies to desired size. You can use a subset of veggies listed.

Cut tofu into cubes and marinade in olive oil, Tamari Sauce, diced Garlic and diced ginger(I usually use a zester and grate the garlic) then cook the tofu at medium high

Combine everything into a bowl
