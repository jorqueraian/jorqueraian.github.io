Ian Jorquera (he/him)

About Me

My name is Ian Jorquera. I am currently pursuing a PhD, as a GEM fellow, in mathematics at Colorado State University advised by Emily J. King. I am also an active participant in the Applied Category Theory seminar and a previous tea time team member.

I graduated from the University of Colorado Boulder with a BA in Computer Science and a BA in Mathematics and a minor in Applied Mathematics.

Research Interests

Frame Theory, Algebraic Combinatorics, Applied Category Theory, Type Theory, and becoming an "algebraist"

My research interests are currently in study of the structure of frames over finite fields. Recently I have been focusing on the matroidal structure of frames over finite fields.

I also just like math, and do math to do math.
